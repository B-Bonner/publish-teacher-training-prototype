Qualification options

You can choose to apply for one of two qualifications with this course.

PGCE with QTS course

With a postgraduate certificate in education (PGCE) with qualified teacher status (QTS) you’ll be able to teach in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. This qualification is also recognised internationally.

QTS only course

Qualified teacher status (QTS) allows you to teach in state schools in England. If you want to teach in the rest of the UK or internationally, then you’ll need a PGCE.
